Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Glen & Dale Outdoor Services ("we," "us," "our") operates the website at gdoutdoorservices.ca and connects homeowners across Saint John, Rothesay, Hampton, and Quispamsis, New Brunswick with vetted local contractors. This privacy policy explains what information we collect when you use this site and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request, contact form, or call our phone line, we collect the information you provide — typically your name, phone number, email address, property location, and details about the service you're requesting. We also collect basic analytics data (pages visited, referring source, approximate location, device type) through Google Analytics to understand how the site is being used.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit to:

  • Match you with a vetted contractor in our network who can quote your project;
  • Follow up with you about your request and confirm the work was completed satisfactorily;
  • Improve the site and our matching process;
  • Comply with applicable Canadian and provincial laws.

Sharing Your Information

To match you with a contractor, we share your inquiry details with the partner contractor we route your job to. We do not sell your information to third parties, marketing brokers, or unrelated advertisers. The contractor receiving your inquiry is bound by our partner agreement to use your details only to provide a quote and perform the requested work.

Phone Calls

Calls to our published phone number may be answered by an AI receptionist and may be recorded or transcribed for quality and routing purposes. If you'd prefer not to have your call recorded, please let us know at the start of the call.

Cookies & Analytics

This site uses cookies for basic functionality and Google Analytics (GA4) to track aggregate site usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without losing functionality.

Your Rights

You can request a copy of the information we hold about you, ask us to correct inaccurate information, or ask us to delete your information by emailing contact@gdoutdoorservices.ca. We'll respond within 30 days. Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA) governs our handling of personal information.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email contact@gdoutdoorservices.ca.
