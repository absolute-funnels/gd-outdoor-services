Landscaping & Sod Services

Whether you're starting fresh after a build or refreshing an overgrown yard, GD Outdoor Services delivers complete landscaping and material services across the greater Saint John region.

🌿
Sod Installation
Proper preparation, leveling, and sod laying for an instant, healthy lawn. Fresh sod delivers immediate results when installed correctly from the ground up.
📐
Lawn Grading & Preparation
Surface leveling, drainage correction, and soil prep before laying sod or seeding — the foundation for a strong, long-lasting lawn.

Gravel Delivery & Spreading
Clean, quality gravel delivered and spread for driveways, pathways, and base work. Improves drainage and stability on any property.

Yard Leveling
Uneven yard? We regrade and level to eliminate low spots, improve drainage, and create a clean, safe, and usable outdoor space.
